BLACK LIVES MATTER IS NOT A TREND

To prevent more black lives from becoming hashtags because of police brutality, we need to help usher in actionable policy changes. To that end, this site is set up to use your best asset – your voice – to begin this change by contacting your statewide officials (we’ve provided a pre-formatted email for you once you click on a representative’s email).

Couple of notes:
The provided template discusses reallocation of municipal budgets, but we urge you to start conversations regarding all racial injustices.

Please edit the email template’s subject title & contribute your own message, beyond the provided email template. We want to ensure that these emails don’t sort into your representatives' spam folders.

Your inputted data is NOT stored & the name field is entirely optional – it is only used for to fill out the email template.

If the site happens to crash, please just reload.

Beyond the context of this site, please consider reading literature on systemic racism, contacting your other local and national representatives, donating, signing petitions, and voting!
